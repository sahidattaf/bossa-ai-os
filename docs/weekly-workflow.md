# BOSSA AI OS — Weekly Workflow

## Objective
Run BOSSA through one consistent weekly operating cycle.

## Weekly cycle

### 1. Update source data
Review and refresh:
- competitors
- menu snapshots
- promos
- pricing control

### 2. Review intelligence
Open:
- 01 — Market & Competitor Intelligence

Identify:
- top threats
- pricing changes
- promo pressure
- menu changes

### 3. Write weekly brief
Open:
- 05 — Weekly AI Briefing

Output:
- executive summary
- top threat
- biggest pricing movement
- most aggressive promo
- recommended BOSSA move
- decision needed

### 4. Review in command center
Open:
- 00 — Executive Command Center

Use the brief to determine:
- what BOSSA should do now
- what BOSSA should ignore
- who owns the action

### 5. Log decision
Open:
- 06 — Decision Log

Capture:
- what was decided
- why it was decided
- owner
- follow-up timing

## Success standard
A good week ends with:
- updated source data
- one real brief
- one clear decision
- one logged follow-up
