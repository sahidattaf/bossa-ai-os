# BOSSA AI OS — Project Walkthrough Script

## 30-Second Summary

BOSSA AI OS is an AI-powered restaurant operating system prototype that connects market intelligence, weekly briefing, decision tracking, and dashboard workflows into one internal system.

It is built to show how restaurant operations can become more structured, decision-ready, and repeatable before adding backend or API automation.

---

## 2-Minute Walkthrough

BOSSA AI OS started from a real operational problem.

Restaurant decisions are often fragmented. Competitor information, campaign ideas, pricing decisions, inventory notes, and staff actions are usually spread across different places.

So I designed BOSSA AI OS as a structured workflow:

Market signals feed into a weekly brief.  
The weekly brief supports executive decisions.  
Decisions become actions.  
Actions are tracked and reviewed.

The dashboard is the visual layer of that system.

It includes:
- KPI cards
- market signals
- weekly brief
- decision tracking
- action panel
- owner grouping
- priority and status badges
- Executive Mode for high-priority focus

The current version uses mock data by design.

The goal was not to rush into APIs first.  
The goal was to validate the workflow, data model, and decision logic first.

Future versions can connect:
- real restaurant data
- Notion databases
- POS or inventory sources
- WhatsApp campaigns
- AI-generated weekly briefs

This project demonstrates AI systems thinking, workflow architecture, product prototyping, and the ability to turn messy business operations into structured internal tools.

---

## Key Message

This is not just a dashboard.

It is a prototype for a repeatable operating system that helps a business think, decide, and act more clearly.

---

## If Someone Asks Why It Matters

A good manager can do many of these tasks manually.

But BOSSA AI OS makes good management more repeatable.

It helps surface problems earlier, suggest actions faster, and keep decisions connected to follow-up.

The value is consistency, timing, and clarity.
