# BOSSA AI OS — Project Overview

## What it is
BOSSA AI OS is an AI-powered restaurant operating system for BOSSA Asado i Mar.

It connects:
- market and competitor intelligence
- weekly executive briefing
- executive decision-making
- decision logging
- AI prompt operations

## Why it exists
The goal is to turn fragmented restaurant thinking into a repeatable operating system.

Instead of random notes and reactions, BOSSA AI OS creates a structured loop:

1. observe the market
2. summarize what matters
3. decide what to do
4. log the decision
5. improve next week

## Core system layers
- 00 — Executive Command Center
- 01 — Market & Competitor Intelligence
- 05 — Weekly AI Briefing
- 06 — Decision Log
- 08 — AI Project Stack & Prompt Pack
