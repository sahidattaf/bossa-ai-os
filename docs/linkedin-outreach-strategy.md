# BOSSA AI OS — LinkedIn Outreach Strategy

## Goal
Turn BOSSA AI OS into real conversations and opportunities.

## Positioning Rule
Do NOT say:
“I am learning AI”

Say:
“I built a working AI-assisted operating system prototype”

## Target Lanes

### Lane 1 — Startups / small teams
- AI startups
- automation teams
- product studios

### Lane 2 — Companies
- hospitality tech
- real estate tech
- workflow platforms
- internal tools teams

Hi [Name] — I’m an AI systems builder from Curaçao focused on workflow architecture and product prototyping.

I recently built BOSSA AI OS, an AI-powered restaurant operating system prototype that connects market intelligence, weekly briefing, decision tracking, and dashboard workflows into one system.

I’m looking to contribute to a serious team building useful AI products. Your work stood out because [reason].

Happy to share the repo and a quick walkthrough.

Hi [Name] — I build AI-powered operating systems and workflow prototypes for real-world business use cases.

One of my projects, BOSSA AI OS, is a structured system for restaurant operations combining intelligence, decisions, and dashboard workflows.

I’m interested in contributing to teams focused on AI workflow design and product systems. I’d be glad to share the project and discuss fit.
